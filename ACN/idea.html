          Science Ideathon brings together teams of people involved in scientific research - including students, researchers, policy makers, publishers,
and funders - to explore how the process of science can be improved. There will be a focus on how open science can accelerate scientific progress
through fostering collaboration ,and reproducibility and by reducing the barriers to learning.
