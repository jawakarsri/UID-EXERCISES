About

          A science hackathon is an event where groups of early career researchers are able to work together on new interdisciplinary projects for a concentrated period of time,
usually over the course of around three days. The aim of a hackathon is to bring together researchers with complementary skills and knowledge;
they then work together to create an initial scientific write-up and research plan, which can provide the momentum needed to get a new collaboration off the ground.
completely independent collaborations with minimal intrusion into their normal duties, and in the process create a basis for long-lasting interdisciplinary research projects.
